About

Graduated from the University of Central Florida in 2011 with a BA in digital media. Studied Character Animation for 2 years, and produced an animated short titled, "Squeaky Business" in 2011. Worked as a sushi chef for 3 years at The Cowfish and 1 year at Morimoto Asia. Born in Boston, Massachusetts and grew up in Boca Raton, Florida.

Hobbies include painting, drawing, cooking, and swimming. Currently learning Web Development at the UCF Coding Bootcamp. Also worked as a graphic designer at a printing company and a production associate at Impress Ink Screen Printing.
